About Me

About Me


Where I'm From

Originally from India, I'm now based in Atlanta, GA after completing my Master's in Technology Management at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (GPA: 3.8/4.0). My path from mechanical engineering to business analysis wasn't linear it was driven by a simple realization: the most impactful solutions start with truly understanding the problem, not jumping to the tool.


How It Started

At Oxigen Services India, I was thrown into a high-stakes environment 5,000+ kiosks processing $6M in transactions daily. When I helped define requirements for a fraud detection system that recovered $300K, I realized I wasn't just running queries, I was shaping how an organization makes decisions. That's when business analysis became more than a job title. It became a craft.


What I Do

I partner with stakeholders across Finance, Operations, and Product teams to identify problems, define requirements, and deliver solutions. Whether it's writing user stories, prioritizing backlogs using MoSCoW, building profitability models, or facilitating sprint planning, I focus on outcomes over outputs. I believe great products emerge when you understand not just what stakeholders ask for, but the underlying problems they're trying to solve.


Domains I've Worked In

Fintech & Risk · E-Commerce & Supply Chain · Education Policy · HR & Workforce Analytics
From validating $6M daily transaction flows to uncovering $200K+ in hidden margin losses, I bring analytical rigor to messy, realworld problems.
